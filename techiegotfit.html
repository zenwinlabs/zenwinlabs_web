What it does

TechieGotFit is a routine-first fitness channel for people whose day is bookended by meetings, not gym time. Every reel is built around a specific slot in a tech worker's day — before the first standup, or after the laptop closes — so the routine fits the job instead of competing with it.

Morning routines

Short, high-output sequences designed to fit before the workday starts — no equipment assumptions, no wasted minutes.

Evening resets

Routines built for after a full day at a desk — undoing the sit, not adding another task to the to-do list.

Built for Bengaluru

Timing, tone, and pacing tuned to the rhythm of corporate tech life in the city — not a generic fitness feed.
