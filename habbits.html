Common questions

Got questions?

Is Habbits really free?
Yes — no ads, no premium tier, no subscription, ever. We believe the best tools shouldn't be paywalled.
Does it work offline?
100% offline. All your data stays on your device. No account needed, no sign-up, no syncing to any server.
Can I export my data?
Yes — export your full habit history as a CSV anytime from the app settings. Your data is always yours to keep.
How many habits can I track?
Unlimited. The app is built to handle as many or as few habits as you want — no artificial caps.
What Android version do I need?
Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher. The app is under 5 MB and optimized for battery and performance.
Will it sync across devices?
Habbits is local-first right now. Optional backup and cross-device sync is on the roadmap for a future update.
